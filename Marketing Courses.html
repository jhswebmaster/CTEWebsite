Marketing Courses

Marketing 1

2 Semesters/1.0 Credit

Available for all grades

CTE Dual Credit

Course Fees: $50 for DECA membership

Taught by: Jennifer Harris


Course Description: This course introduces students to marketing concepts taught in college and university business and marketing programs. It utilizes project-based learning to help students understand and apply the functions of marketing and the infrastructure of the business world. Topics include:

  • Selling
  • Consumer behavior
  • 5 P's of marketing
  • Business environment
  • Ethical practices
  • Business Ownership
  • Management
  • Communication
  • Principles of economics

Students also get the opportunity to challenge themself academically and develop practical business experience through DECA competitions and conferences. This course may be applied toward the JHS Business and Marketing Certificate.

Marketing 2

2 Semesters/1.0 Credit

Grades 10 11 12

CTE Dual Credit

Course Fees: 50$ for DECA membership

Taught by: Jennifer Harris


Course Description: This course teaches marketing concepts, skills and the business foundations required for understanding the development of marketing. Students will be able to demonstrate their understanding of sales and marketing concepts through project-based learning. Students also get the opportunity to practice their business skills through the DECA competition, which they will prepare in class. This is a 0 period class. This course may be applied toward the JHS Business and Marketing Certificate.
